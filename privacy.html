FabTech Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25th July 2024

What Information Do We Collect?

We collect the following types of personal data:

  • Contact Information: Your name, email address, phone number, and mailing address.
  • Order Information: Your order history, including the products you've purchased and the payment methods you've used.
  • Device Information: Information about the device you use to access our website, including your IP address, browser type, and operating system.
  • Usage Information: Information about how you use our website, including the pages you visit and the actions you take.

How Do We Use Your Information?

We use your information to:

  • Process Your Orders: We use your contact and order information to process and fulfill your orders.
  • Improve Our Website: We use your device and usage information to improve the functionality and user experience of our website.
  • Communicate with You: We use your contact information to communicate with you about your orders, promotions, and other updates.
  • Personalize Your Experience: We use your usage information to personalize your experience on our website, including recommending products that may be of interest to you.

How Do We Share Your Information?

We share your information with:

  • Payment Processors: We share your payment information with our payment processors to facilitate transactions.
  • Shipping Providers: We share your contact and order information with our shipping providers to fulfill your orders.
  • Marketing Partners: We share your contact information with our marketing partners to send you promotions and other updates.
  • Law Enforcement: We may share your information with law enforcement agencies if we believe it's necessary to comply with the law or protect our rights.

How Do We Protect Your Information?

We take the following measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: We use encryption to protect your payment information and other sensitive data.
  • Access Controls: We limit access to your information to authorized personnel only.
  • Secure Servers: We store your information on secure servers that are protected by firewalls and other security measures.

Your Rights

You have the following rights:

  • Access: You have the right to access your information and request corrections or updates.
  • Deletion: You have the right to request that we delete your information.
  • Opt-Out: You have the right to opt-out of marketing communications and other updates.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If we make any changes, we'll post them on this page and notify you by email or other means.
